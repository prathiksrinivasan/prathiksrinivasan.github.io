OnlyGrabs

Unity, C# OnlyGrabs is a 1v1 simple 2D fighting game. Themed around wrestling, players can use a combination of grabs, lunges, and dodges to predict and outplay their opponent. This prototype was solo-developed in 2 weeks.

Key Takeaways

- Gained experience in designing and developing a 1v1 combat system from scratch
- Learned how to design collision and hitboxes to make interactions feel fair and responsive
- Managed 'frame data' and timing of moves to balance combat system
Hitbox and hurtbox editor
