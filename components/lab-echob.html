Lab Echo

Unreal Engine 5, Blueprints, C++ Lab Echo is a narrative-focused adventure and puzzle solving game, where players are tasked with solving the mystery behind the abandoned lab they find themselves in. This project was created over the course of 4 months as a capstone project, with a team of 7.

Project Responsibilities

- Conceptualized and refined overall game design and mechanics, including secondary puzzle system
- Developed level layouts and puzzle structure
- Conducted playtests and refined content based on feedback and observations
- Created game narrative and integrated with puzzle elements
- Created game trailer
