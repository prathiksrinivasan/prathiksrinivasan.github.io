BitBoxers

C BitBoxers is a 1v1 fighting game developed on the Arm Cortex Microcontroller. Taking inspiration from fighting games and autobattlers, players queue up a sequence of attacks with unique interactions until only one is left standing. This project was created with a team of 5 over the course of 2 months.

Project Responsibilities

- Conceptualized overall game design and flow, including physical control requirements and interactions
- Programmed overall combat game loop and AI opponent system
- Programmed graphics drivers for displaying sprites on LCD screen
